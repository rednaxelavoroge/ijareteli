One of One · Forever

The Original Is Burned

Once a painting is issued as a digital original, Phridon Bolkvadze commits the physical original to fire. No print, no copy, no canvas in a vault survives — the digital original becomes the single, final form of the work. Its owner holds not a reproduction, but the only version that will ever exist.

An Original Art Movement

COFEXSIZM

COFEXSIZM is an art movement that Phridon Bolkvadze created and named himself. His works are painted with natural coffee and coffee grounds, whose pigment yields warm, earthy tones and a living texture that cannot be reproduced. No such movement yet exists officially in the world — it is the artist's own term, and each work remains unique, a single original.
