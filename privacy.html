This notice explains how ijareteli.com (MODA ARTS · Phridon Bolkvadze “Ijareteli”) uses cookies and similar technologies.

What we collect

  • Page views, interactions with artworks (gallery / NFT lightbox), and contact actions (email, phone, WhatsApp).

  • Technical data needed for security and measurement (IP address on the server for Meta Conversions API, browser user-agent).

Cookies & consent

We ask for your consent before enabling analytics and advertising storage (Google Consent Mode v2). You can choose Accept all, Analytics only, or Reject. Your choice is stored in the browser (ij_consent_v2).

Partners

  • Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager — traffic analytics and tag delivery.
  • Meta Pixel and Meta Conversions API — ad measurement and optimization (with event deduplication).

These services process data according to their own policies. Ads are not “launched” by this site; they are managed in Google Ads / Meta Ads accounts separately.
